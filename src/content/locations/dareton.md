Dareton is a small NSW border town in the Wentworth Shire, about twenty minutes from central Mildura. It serves the surrounding agricultural district and has the look and feel of a long-settled Sunraysia town.

The housing stock is typical of small Sunraysia agricultural towns — fibro cottages, post-war weatherboards, modest brick veneers on generous blocks. There isn't much new construction, and most homes have been in their current form for decades. Block sizes are above suburban average, and the streetscape retains a small-town character.
