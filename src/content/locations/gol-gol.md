Gol Gol sits directly across the river from Mildura on the NSW side, ten minutes from the Mildura CBD via the bridge. Historically a small irrigation settlement, the town has grown rapidly over the past two decades as estate developments expanded to meet demand from buyers wanting Mildura proximity without Victorian prices.

The housing reflects that recent growth. Most of the stock is between five and twenty years old — modern project-home estates with single and double-storey four-bedroom builds, rendered cement-sheet façades, and the look of fast-paced suburban development. There are still pockets of older riverside cottages and rural-residential blocks, but the dominant housing here is contemporary estate-style.
