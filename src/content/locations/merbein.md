Merbein is one of the smaller Sunraysia towns, about fifteen minutes north-west of Mildura, settled as part of the same irrigation push that established Mildura. Its history is built around dried-fruit production, citrus and grape blocks, and the town retains a quieter, semi-rural character.

Housing here is predominantly mid-century brick veneer and weatherboard on generous blocks, with a handful of newer infill builds and a fair number of older properties that have been added to over the decades. The kind of large flat block that's harder to find in central Mildura is still common in Merbein.
