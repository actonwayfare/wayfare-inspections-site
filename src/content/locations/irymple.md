Irymple sits about twelve minutes south-east of central Mildura, traditionally a vine and citrus growing area within the irrigation district. As blocks have been subdivided over the years, Irymple has grown from a small farming settlement into an established satellite suburb.

The housing reflects that history. Brick-veneer family homes from the 1970s and 80s sit on the original residential streets, with pockets of older weatherboards and a steady supply of newer four-bedroom builds on subdivided vine blocks closer to the Calder Highway. Generous block sizes are still the norm in the older parts of town.
