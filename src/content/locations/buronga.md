Buronga sits on the NSW side of the Murray, just across the bridge from Mildura. Historically a small riverside community, the town has grown over the past two decades into a fast-developing residential suburb supplying housing for the broader Mildura workforce.

The housing mix is dominated by modern estate-style builds — single and double-storey project homes on tight modern blocks — alongside a quieter layer of older riverside cottages near the bank and on the lower-lying streets. The pace of development through the 2000s and 2010s gives Buronga a distinctly newer feel than Mildura proper.
